Here are some interesting facts about chocolate from Wikipedia

  • Cocoa beans were often used as currency by the Aztecs.
  • The word "chocolate" entered the English language from Spanish in about 1600.
  • The Aztecs associated chocolate with Quetzalcoatl, who, according to one legend, was cast away by the other gods for sharing chocolate with humans.
